Overview

Purpose

Our objective at White Water Rafting is to become the leading provider of thrilling and safe white water rafting experiences in our region, delivering unforgettable adventures for our customers. We aim to double our customer base and increase market presence over the next two years while maintaining high standards of safety, sustainability, and customer satisfaction on every trip

Audience

The target audience for "White Water Rafting" includes adventurous individuals aged 18-35 who seek excitement and enjoy outdoor activities and water sports. Friend groups and corporate teams aged 25-45 are also a target, looking for thrilling experiences to strengthen bonds and enhance teamwork. Additionally, adventure-seeking tourists aged 25-50, keen on unique vacation experiences and exploring the region, are part of our focus. Lastly, parents and families aged 30-50 looking for safe yet exciting outdoor activities to enjoy and create lasting memories together are also within our target audience. Customized marketing and services will cater to the preferences and needs of these groups.
